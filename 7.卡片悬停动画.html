Fantasy

the second studio album by Chinese Taiwan singer Jay Chou, released on 14 September 2001 by BMG Taiwan.[1] The album was nominated for ten Golden Melody Awards and won five awards, including Best Pop Vocal Album, Best Album Producer, and Best Composer.[2][3] The album also won for an IFPI Hong Kong Top Sales Music Award for Top 10 Best Selling Mandarin Albums of the Year.[4]
